Process

1. Prepare the Meat Sauce
Heat olive oil in a large skillet or Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Add the ground beef, Italian sausage, and diced onion. Cook, breaking the meat into small pieces with a wooden spoon, until browned and no longer pink, about 8-10 minutes. Drain excess fat.
Add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, Italian seasoning, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat to low and let it simmer for at least 30 minutes, stirring occasionally. 
2. Cook the Noodles
While the sauce simmers, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
Add the lasagna noodles and cook according to package directions until al dente (firm).
Drain the noodles and rinse them with cold water to prevent sticking. Lay them flat on parchment paper. 
3. Make the Cheese Mixture
In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, beaten egg, chopped parsley, 1/4 cup of the Parmesan, 1 cup of the mozzarella, 1/4 tsp salt, and 1/8 tsp pepper. Mix well. 
4. Assemble the Lasagna
Preheat your oven to 375째F (190째C). Lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
Spread about 1 cup of the meat sauce evenly across the bottom of the dish to prevent the noodles from sticking.
Arrange a layer of 3-4 noodles over the sauce, overlapping slightly if needed.
Spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture over the noodles. Top with about 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce, and sprinkle with some of the remaining mozzarella.
Repeat the layers twice more: noodles, ricotta mixture, meat sauce, and mozzarella.
Finish with a final layer of noodles, the remaining meat sauce, remaining mozzarella cheese, and the remaining Parmesan cheese.
